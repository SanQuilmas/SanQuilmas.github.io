What I'm Doing Now

Updated: June 2025

Work

I'm currently working as a Full-Stack Developer at Deloitte ADMX, contributing to enterprise-level solutions using modern web technologies like React, Spring Boot, Nest, Next and containerized backends.

Learning

I'm deepening my knowledge of Rust for backend services, exploring system-level programming and async patterns. I'm also getting more familiar with Kubernetes for production deployments.

I'm also dedicating time to improving my musicianship! Practicing my scales, refining technique, and deepening my understanding of music theory whenever I can.

Side Projects

I'm actively improving ReMusic 2.0, my tool for digitizing sheet music, with better OCR accuracy and usability. I'm also maintaining a music archive for Tuna ENSJ.

Life

Living in Guadalajara, staying active and practicing music. Trying to keep a healthy balance between work and personal growth.
